About Our Company

Trusted Builder
Since 2010

Syarikat Pembinaan M.R is a licensed and registered construction company based in Sarawak, Malaysia. We have built a strong reputation through years of dedication to quality workmanship and timely delivery — serving both public and private sector clients across the region.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to high standards of construction, renovation, and civil works — ensuring every project reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.
